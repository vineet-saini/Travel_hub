Shimla Hill Retreat - 4 Days
=====================================
Day 1: Arrival & Mall Road Stroll
- Reach Shimla; check into heritage hotel.
- Walk Mall Road – shop for woolens, visit Christ Church.
- Evening: Ridge light show.

Day 2: Toy Train to Kufri
- UNESCO toy train ride (1 hr) to Kufri hills.
- Horse riding, yak rides, nature trails.
- Lunch at hill cafe; return for high tea.

Day 3: Jakhu Temple & Viceregal Lodge
- Hike to Jakhu Temple (Hanuman statue view).
- Tour Viceregal Lodge (British Raj history).
- Evening free: Scandal Point cafe hop.

Day 4: Annandale & Departure
- Morning picnic at Annandale grounds.
- Transfer to station/airport.

=====================================
Price: ₹35,000/person
Inclusions: Meals, transport, stays.
Exclusions: Shopping, adventure extras.
Best Time: Oct-March (cool winters).
