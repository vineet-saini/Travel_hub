Agra Taj Magic - 3 Days
=====================================
Day 1: Taj Mahal Sunrise
- Arrive; early Taj visit (less crowds, golden light).
- Mehtab Bagh gardens for sunset views.
- Stay: Riverside hotel.

Day 2: Agra Fort & Fatehpur Sikri
- Explore Agra Fort (Mughal fortress).
- Drive to Fatehpur Sikri (abandoned city, Buland Darwaza).
- Evening: Light & sound show at Taj.

Day 3: Local Markets & Departure
- Marble inlay shopping (pietra dura).
- Transfer to Delhi/Agra airport.
