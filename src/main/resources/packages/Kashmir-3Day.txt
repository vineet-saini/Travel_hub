Kashmir Paradise Tour - 3 Days
=====================================
Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar
- Check into luxurious houseboat on Dal Lake.
- Evening shikara ride at sunset – enjoy floating markets and lotus blooms.
- Dinner: Traditional Kashmiri Wazwan feast (lamb rogan josh, gushtaba).

Day 2: Gulmarg Adventure
- Drive to Gulmarg (1.5 hrs) – world's highest gondola ride to Apharwat Peak.
- Snow sledding or pony rides in meadows (seasonal).
- Return to Srinagar; visit Mughal Gardens (Shalimar Bagh).

Day 3: Pahalgam Valley & Departure
- Head to Pahalgam (2 hrs) – Betaab Valley trek, apple orchard picnic.
- Lidder River rafting (optional thrill).
- Evening transfer to airport; farewell with saffron tea.
